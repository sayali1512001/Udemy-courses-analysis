Findings of Our Research

  • "AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate 2020" course has maximum no. of subscribers and also good reviews from the user.
  • There are less no of free courses available than the paid courses under the it-software category.
  • On an average when the num_reviews decreases then the num_subscriberes also decreases and when the num_reviews increases then the num_subscribers also increases.
  • When the average of discount_price_amount increases then the average rating and average price_detailed_amount also increases and when the the average of discount_price_amount decreases then the average rating and average price_detailed_amount also decreases.
  • When the num_reviews of the course goes up then the discount _price_amount of the course increases.
  • In 2016 and before that, more lectures are published and as the year progresses the less no. of lectures are published.
